FAQs

Which apps does ionXtech build?

We build Android utility apps focused on productivity and simplicity, including QR Solutions, LED Scroller, and Auto Call Scheduler.

Is QR Solutions free?

QR Solutions is free to download. Some features may be supported by ads or optional upgrades depending on the app store version.

Why does QR Solutions need camera permission?

The camera permission is used only to scan QR codes and barcodes. Images are processed on-device and not sent to our servers.

Does LED Scroller collect my data?

No. The text you type is processed locally. If ads are enabled, ad providers may collect device identifiers for ad delivery.

Why does Auto Call Scheduler request call permissions?

It uses call-related permissions to automate scheduled calls you create. Your call history and contacts remain on your device.

Do your apps work offline?

Most core features work offline. Any ads, updates, or store-related features require an internet connection.

How do I delete my data?

App data is stored on your device. You can clear app data from your Android settings or uninstall the app.

Where can I find the privacy policy?

Visit the Privacy Policy page to read each app's policy.

I have a billing or refund question.

All payments and refunds are handled by Google Play. Please contact Google Play support for billing issues.

Email us at ionxtechsolution@gmail.com. We typically respond within 2-3 business days.
